Who We Are

After over 20 years in the pet industry and 15 years in the aquarium industry we realised that we have many people who require fresh new ideas and new breeds of fish to keep the hobby exciting.

As fellow hobbyists ourselves we have been having the same problem...

The conventional pet shop has a wide variety of animals and are usually static, we however are not. We are specialists, the kind that aquarists are constantly looking for...

Well look no further!

By focusing solely on fish, we can bring in new breeds from all over the world! Thus being dynamic and keeping the hobby exciting.

Why keep dreaming of fish you could have? Pay us a visit and see for yourself!

Marine Fish & Corals

We stock a large variety of Marine Livestock consisting of Fish And Corals that is diverse and constantly changing as we receive stock once per week.

All of our stock is imported from across the world from only the best suppliers to ensure that we have the best and most unique specimens available.

Freshwater Fish

We specialise in freshwater fish such as a wide variety of Cichlids and Discus.

All of our stock is imported from reputable suppliers and we also stock locally bred stock to support reputable local suppliers.

We usually receive new shipments once per month.
